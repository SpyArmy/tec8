Nestled between the ancient pines and the soothing shores, Wolfridge Wilderness is a sanctuary where the wild meets the serene. Whether you're seeking adventure or tranquility, our haven offers something for everyone.

Timber Wolves (Canis lupus)

Timber wolves are known for their impressive communication skills. They use a variety of vocalizations, body language, and scent marking to communicate with each other.

Clownfish (Amphiprioninae)

Clownfish have a fascinating ability to change sex. All clownfish are born male, and they have the ability to change into a female if the dominant female in their group dies.

Eastern White Pine (Pinus strobus)

This majestic tree is known for its tall, straight trunk and soft, feathery needles. It's a key species in the forest, providing habitat and shelter for various wildlife, including birds and small mammals.
